about

xin DJs, produces, writes, reads, grows and codes...

they may take the form of a meat wad, pile of trash or smallish person.

2025's WASTED (Appendix.files) is composed of field recordings of waste, composts cultural leftovers into new sonic growths. it is accompanied by essays 'hospicing dance music' and 'unsustainability' which reflect on exhaustion and cultural afterlives.

their 2019 record, MELTS INTO LOVE, was released biodigitally through Subtext Recordings. on it, they sculpt neurofunk, hardcore and dubstep into a caustic, amorphous glob of reese basses and pseudo-breaks.

in 2022-23 they tunneled wormholes through possible worlds and their musics on Aaja Radio.

expect all good things wiggy, drippy and weird.
